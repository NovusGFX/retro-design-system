On silence,
in interfaces.

One color. One font. A single line. Space is the most persuasive design element.

Every pixel you place is a commitment to make the reader notice. Every pixel you withhold is an act of trust. This page trusts you.

When a thousand things distract, one thing remembered is the loudest.

The monochrome page is not austere. It is generous. It hands the reader back their attention, undivided. Nothing blinks. Nothing slides. Nothing insists. The type does the arguing; the page holds still.
